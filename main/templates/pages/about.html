How to Apply for Schemes

Follow the steps below to apply for government schemes available for tribal communities.

Step-by-Step Process

  1. Visit the official government portal for your selected scheme.
  2. Register using your mobile number, email, or Aadhaar details.
  3. Fill in all the required personal and family details.
  4. Upload the necessary documents such as ID proof, caste certificate, income certificate, etc.
  5. Submit the form and note the application ID for tracking.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Caste Certificate (Tribal)
  • Income Certificate
  • Bank Passbook
  • Address Proof
  • Passport-size Photograph
