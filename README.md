Adivasi Bandhu is a web-based platform developed to support Adivasi (tribal) communities by providing easy access to government schemes, welfare information, and support services through a simple and user-friendly interface.

The project focuses on digital inclusion, awareness, and accessibility for tribal people, students, women, workers, and elders.
